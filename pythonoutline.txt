------------
Python RPS
------------

Steps:

	1 - player picks a weapon
	2 - cpu cooses a weapon
	3 - compare the two choices
	4 - decide winner
	5 - remove life from loser
	6 - end game when player wins twice
	7 - quit game

Framework:

	- quit at any time
	- cpu is a random choice
	- cpu draws after player
	- display message for win/loss
	- clickable weapon choice
	- continue button shown after round
	- two rounds is a win
	- three rounds total
	- display score
